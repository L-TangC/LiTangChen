Mcoin Offical Web

Design Lead / Flow / Prototype

This website is developed specifically to introduce our team's newly launched M Coin, providing investors with comprehensive information about this cryptocurrency. Designed for maximum efficiency, it enables investors to complete necessary steps with minimal effort, while clearly understanding the current phase of the coin's release. Additionally, the website features captivating 3D visualizations of M Coin and various interactive elements, offering users a visually engaging experience.

Difficulties

As the design lead for this project, my role extended beyond UI/UX tasks to orchestrating the integration of our team's 3D and 2D artists' contributions. This multifaceted responsibility required forward-thinking and offered a prime opportunity to master the art of balancing and enhancing my colleagues' designs. It also taught me the importance of fostering inspiration and maintaining passion within the team.

The primary challenge of this project was establishing a distinctive brand image that exudes both tranquility and uniqueness. Recognizing the need to overhaul our cryptocurrency coin's layout was a pivotal moment. This redesign not only aligned with our branding objectives but also empowered the marketing team to more effectively promote our product.

Wireframe

Continuing from our previous discussions on the flash claiming website, we are progressing well and are on the right path. The next critical step is to develop an official claiming website, enabling users to claim with ease.

The design of the landing page, as the official website, necessitates a meticulous approach to displaying functions and structuring the content blocks with precision. It's essential to organize the information in a way that users can effortlessly locate their desired tasks. Moreover, the design should enhance the user experience, captivating users' interest in exploring and claiming other coins. This strategic layout not only facilitates user engagement but also strengthens the overall effectiveness of the claiming process.

Crypto Coin Re-design

In the brainstorming phase for Mcoin, I pinpointed keywords that encapsulate its essence and could serve as its symbolic markers.

  • M for ModelTV
    The letter "M" stands as a direct nod to "ModelTV," the parent company, embedding its identity within the coin's design.
  • Enchantment and Nature
    Drawing inspiration from the mystical and natural realms, "mushroom" and "magical" were selected as keywords. These elements are echoed in the coin's aesthetic, which features flowing motions to evoke a psychedelic, captivating effect.
  • Age Evolution in Web3
    To represent the dynamic and evolving nature of the Web3 industry, the design of each coin melds elements from two distinct eras, blending materials and motifs. This fusion symbolizes the crossing of ages, reflecting the revolutionary trajectory of technology and society.
